## Common questions

**Is my data private?**
Yes. Kcalm stores everything **on your device** — no accounts, no servers, no ads, no tracking. See our [Privacy Policy](./).

**How do I log food?**
Open the **Log** tab and speak your meal ("2 eggs and a coffee for breakfast"), scan a barcode, or type it.

**A food's nutrition looks off?**
Open it in **Settings → Food Database**, tap the food, and edit the values — or use **"Enter from label"** to convert a package's per-serving numbers to per-100 g/mL automatically.

**How do I add a family member?**
**Settings → Family Members → +** to create a profile, each with its own goals, photo, and history.

**How do I track health metrics or medications?**
Open **Body & Health** — add readings (weight, body fat, glucose, ketones, or your own custom metrics like blood-test results) and log medication doses.

**How do I switch to Dark mode?**
**Settings → Appearance → Dark.**
