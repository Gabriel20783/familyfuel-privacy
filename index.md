# Kcalm — Privacy Policy

*Effective date: July 5, 2026*

Kcalm ("the app") is a nutrition and health tracker for you and your family. We designed it to be **private by default**: your data stays on your device.

## Data we collect

None. Kcalm has no accounts, no servers, and no analytics, advertising, or tracking. We do not collect, transmit, or sell any personal or health information.

## Where your data lives

All the information you enter — profiles, meals, food entries, exercise, body/health metrics, and reports — is stored **only on your device** (via Apple's on-device database). PINs are stored in the device **Keychain**. Uninstalling the app removes this data; it may be included in your own iCloud/iTunes device backups, which you control.

## Third-party services

- **Open Food Facts** — when you look up a food by name or barcode, the app sends only that **search term or barcode** (no personal identifiers) to openfoodfacts.org to retrieve nutrition data. See [openfoodfacts.org](https://openfoodfacts.org) for their terms.
- **Apple Speech Recognition** — voice logging converts your speech to text using Apple's speech recognition. On devices that support it, this happens **entirely on-device**; otherwise audio is processed by Apple under Apple's privacy policy. The app keeps only the resulting text, locally.

## Permissions

- **Microphone** and **Speech Recognition** are used only for voice food logging.
- **Face ID / Touch ID** is used only to unlock the app if you enable App Lock.

Each permission is optional and requested only when you first use the related feature.

## Children

Family profiles (including children) are stored only on your device. Because we collect no data, no personal information from children is transmitted or shared.

## Changes

We'll update this policy here if the app's data practices change.
